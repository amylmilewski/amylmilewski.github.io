StudyLab

StudyLab is a a full-stack (React + Strapi/GraphQL) study session tracker application where users log entries of their study sessions and reflect back on what strategies worked well for them by filtering views of their entries by factors like time of day, stress level, and overall success rating.

Getting Started

Users can log their study sessions, recording the date, time of day, start and end time, stress level, setting, success rating, and description of what you did (like how focused you felt, strategies you tried, or background distractions).

Scroll through your study sessions — each one is neatly summarized as a card. Click on a card to open the full session details.

Use the filters at the top to explore patterns:

  • Narrow by time of day (morning, afternoon, evening).
  • Select a stress level or rating range.
  • Apply a date range to see progress over time.
  • You can use one filter at a time, or combine multiple to dig deeper.

Reflect on what works best — maybe you notice you study better in the morning with low stress and in a quiet setting.
