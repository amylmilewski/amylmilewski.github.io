Build-a-Workout

Build-a-Workout is a full-stack web application designed to help you create and organize your own custom workout routines.

Getting Started

After logging in, users can visit the "My Exercises" page and view the collection of any exercises you have already created. In the "Add a New Exercise" form on the right side of the screen, you can add new exercises, specifying the name of the exercise, the number of sets, the number of reps per set, and the weight/load (in lbs). After clicking "Add Exercise", the new exercise card will appear to the left.

On the "My Routines" page, users can build custom routines. The exercises you have added to your account appear in a multi-select field in the routine creation form. After selecting the desired exercises and naming the routine, clicking the "Create Routine" button will produce a new routine card.

Tip: A full Getting Started guide is available on the home page after you create an account.
